📋 QUICK COMMANDS:

1. View Help:
   .followar

2. Add Auto-Reply:
   .followar add follow|Thanks for following! 🎉

3. List All Replies:
   .followar list

4. Remove Reply:
   .followar remove follow

5. Clear All:
   .followar clear
